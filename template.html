Precautions for Different Dementia Classes

Class Precautions
Demented 1. Consult a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.
2. Ensure a safe and supportive environment for the individual.
3. Monitor and manage their daily activities.
Mild Demented 1. Consult a specialist for early intervention.
2. Provide cognitive stimulation and engage in memory-enhancing activities.
3. Ensure a structured daily routine.
Moderate Demented 1. Seek professional medical care and treatment.
2. Ensure a secure and supervised living arrangement.
3. Focus on comfort and quality of life.
Very Mild Demented 1. Consult a healthcare provider for cognitive assessment.
2. Encourage mental and social engagement.
3. Monitor and adapt as the condition progresses.
